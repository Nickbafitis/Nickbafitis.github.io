Stacking Tutorial

what you will need for this tutorial

  • camera
  • lens
  • tripod
  • computer
  • weather app
  • star finding app
  • dew heater (if it is humid)

    • imaging


    choose you deep sky object and wait until a clear day (preferably with a new moon)
    and take your images and if you can your calibration frames

    processing


    once you have taken your images use a processing software like siril
    or pixinsight to process and stack your images(make sure you take at least 100 images of your object)

    enjoying your final result

    once you are done processing you can print your image or publish it on an online forum like astrobin and you're done!

    Best stacking softwares for astrophotography

    1. pixinsight
    2. siril (recommended)
    3. photoshop
    4. sequator
    5. deep sky stacker
